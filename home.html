How To Play

Regal, egotistical, and full of pride, Vegeta was once a ruthless, cold-blooded warrior and outright killer, but later abandons his role in the Frieza Force, opting to remain alive on Earth. His character evolves from villain, to anti-hero, then to god through the course of the game. The result of each corresponding button pressed will increment Vegeta's target value. Press 'Attack' for Big Bang Attack, press 'Speak' button to make Vegeta talk. If any values go to 0 Vegeta will go to sleep and you lost the game. **WARNING**
Vegeta's insults cause numbers to go down. Reach 10 to move to the next level
