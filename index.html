Welcome to VegetaTamagotchi

Pet Vegeta

Start off when Vegeta was a kid and progress on each level by using Vegeta as a Tamagotchi. This regal, egotistical, and full of pride, Vegeta was once a ruthless, cold-blooded warrior and outright killer, but with your help, YOU can turn him into the character fans know and love

How To Play

Intro

Vegetatamagotchi has three levels, to clear each level and progress throughout the game you must fill these categories

Workout
Happy
Pride

Clicking

Every category has a corresponding button, each click will result in one value in the progress bar

Beware of red buttons they will hinder you to make progress

Attack

Starting at zero for each level you will have to race against time and avoid clicking the red buttons to fill Vegetas Pride progress bar

When full Big Bang Attack can be used!

Yelling

The progress bar will lose one value every time Vegeta yells at you.

If a category progress bar is empty the game will end

WARNING
Turn down volume
